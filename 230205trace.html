TRACE A discount tracking app for clothing.

Buying clothes during sale seasons is a great way to save money, but it comes with many challenges. Product styles and sizes are limited and popular items sell out quickly. It is also difficult to keep track of when or where sales are happening as they occur at different times of the year and vary in duration. How can we better connect people with discounted clothing?

A Discount Tracking App for Selective Clothing Shoppers

TRACE is a mobile app that helps shoppers keep track of stores and items on sale. The app notifies you when a store or item is on sale and takes into account different type of sales - flash sale or clearance sale - and how much an item is discounted.

TRACE also takes a unique approach to discount tracking. Whereas traditional discount tracking apps focus on comparing prices from many stores and feature a large range of products, this app prioritizes tracking items and stores on sale and not on comparing prices across multiple stores.

TRACE provides a more optimal experience for those who only shop for clothing, have a limited number of preferred stores, shop at small independent stores, or canâ€™t find products they want on other discount tracking apps.

User Research

Surveys and interviews showed that people encountered numerous points of difficulty when they attempted to buy items on sale.

Making people aware that there is a sale going on is simply the first hurdle amongst many. The higher the value the more competition, and the price can fluctuate unpredictably. There is also the element of time sensitvity: different issues arise at different points of the purchasing timeline.

Homepage
